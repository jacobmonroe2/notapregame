The Pregame

The Pregame is a creative collective that brings people together in unconventional, casual spaces before they go out at night, hence the name, The Pregame. More than just a party, it's a cultural experience — blending music, community, and visual storytelling.

Conceived by Che Ewell, Howard DJ "justche," The Pregame started organically: friends coming together, live DJ sets, and good energy before going out. Now, the goal is to bring that same intimate vibe to unique spaces across the city and beyond.

Each event is captured through professional videography, showcasing not just the DJs but the spaces themselves — highlighting their character, atmosphere, and the people who fill them.
